Overview

Dikenocracy is not only a theory. It is also an engineering and public-infrastructure effort expressed through concrete tools and experiments. Each project implements a bounded part of the protocol stack in verifiable, testable software.

Active projects

Converter DTI

A deterministic converter between Gregorian, Hebrew, and Dikenocratic Time Interface (DTI) formats. Implements DKP-0-TIME-001 with a canonical JDN-based domain layer, FastAPI service, and Streamlit client.
